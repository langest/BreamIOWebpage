Mission

Why is Bream IO here? To change the way we create, play and experience games. Bream IO is the brainchild of a group of young engineers from Stockholm, Sweden who have set out to be part of the future of games. With the increased ubiquity of technology and advances in biometrics the Bream IO team passionately believes that by integrating human senses and control with computers we will be able to create truly immersive and exiting gaming experiences for everyone. How? As a first step towards reaching this, the Bream IO team has chosen to focus its time and energy on unleashing the power of your eyes within the realm of gaming. Through cutting edge eye-tracking technology Bream IO allows developers, players and spectators to experience games like never before. Developers are able to easily and intuitively integrate eye-tracking interactivity into game development and deliver truly immersive gaming experiences. Players are able to interact and control games at a completely new level resulting in a more compelling and active gaming experience. Spectators are presented with a whole new way of experiencing e-sports and come even closer to the games themselves, resulting in an unparalleled understanding of what is happening in the driver seat. So, what does Bream IO offer? Bream IO started out by creating EyeStream. EyeStream is an easy to use and easy to integrate eye-tracking software suite specifically engineered to bring e-sports spectators closer to the games. By tracking gamers' eyes while they challenge each other on the virtual battlefield, EyeStream allows spectators to follow their every eye movement and see what they see. EyeStream also brings with it state of the art analytics functionality so both commentators and spectators can see relevant data regarding individual gamers’ eye movements throughout a given game. EyeStream ultimately aims to take e-sports to the next level by showing whats off the screen, onto it. Read more…(clickable) Bream IO is also working on its next project, EyeO. EyeO is a middleware software that will allow developers to intuitively and easily integrate eye-tracking capabilities and functionalities into their games. Through a carefully engineered Unity-plugin, developers will be able to use intuitive code commands to allow players eye-tracking functionalities that will allow for truly immersive control over any type of game. EyeO will make integrating next generation gaming control simple and hassle-free. EyeO will also allow games with eye-tracking capabilities to be cross-compatible to any market eye-tracking device, making it the go-to middleware for game-developers. Read more…(clickable)
