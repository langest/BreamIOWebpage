Eye tracking for games

Mission

Bream IO is developing Eye tracking software. Our focus is set on the gaming industry where we believe eye tracking technology will provide a new level of immersion. This both through new ways of interacting with games and through revolutionary ways of visualizing e-sport practitioners actions. Sign up for our newsletter for updates on our progress in bringing eye tracking into gaming.
