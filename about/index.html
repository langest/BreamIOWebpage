About

Who we are

Bream IO is the brainchild of a group of young engineers from Stockholm, Sweden who have set out to be part of the future of games. The Bream IO team passionately believes that by integrating human senses and control with computers, we will be able to create truly immersive and exciting gaming experiences for everyone. The team has chosen to focus its time and energy on unleashing the power of your eyes within the realm of gaming. Through cutting edge eye-tracking technology Bream IO allows developers, players and spectators to experience games like never before.
